Sam K. Baseley

I'm Sam, an aspiring videogame developer and designer. I found my calling very early in life at the age of six, when I tried to put two disks into my Playstation 2 at the same time. Thinking this would create a new game that would comprise of both aspects of the games I put in...

Obviously both the disks were ruined and I was left with nothing to show for my valiant experiment. However, my curiosity and desire to make games never diminished. Now days I don't often resort to such destructive methods of making games though.

Samuel Kenneth Baseley,
Games programmer & designer.
